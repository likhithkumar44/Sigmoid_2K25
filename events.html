Sigmoid Events

Explore our diverse range of events and competitions

Techfusion

A Techfusion is an event where participants submit and present research papers for evaluation..

Posterize

A poster presentation is an event where participants display and present their research on a visual poster..

Innovista

Innovista is an event where participants display innovative DIY projects and showcase technical creativity..

Techcrix

TECHCRIX is an event where participants solve circuit questions and build models to test their skills..

Quiztronics

ElectroQuiz is a three-round quiz that challenges participants on electronics, current affairs, and emerging technologies, with scores represented as "Bits."

CodeVerse

A contest with competetive coding that challenges programming to test their algorithmic thinking and coding skills.

Click Fest

CLICK FEST is a photography contest where participants capture creative images based on themes, showcasing technical skills and unique perspectives of daily life.

DumbCharades and Pictionary

DUMDCHARADES AND PICTIONARY is a two-level game featuring Dumbcharades and Pictionary, testing acting, observation, and drawing skills.

Kim's Game

KIM'S GAME is a 3-level memory game where participants identify missing or misplaced objects to advance through rounds.

Guess Busters

Guess Busters is a two-round game where players guess phrases and movie-related clues.

MOBILE ASSEMBLING

A mobile assembly workshop focuses on smartphone hardware, component assembly, troubleshooting, and repair techniques.

ASTRONOMY

An astronomy workshop explores celestial bodies, space phenomena, observation techniques, and the fundamentals of the universe.

Iot Workshop

IoT Workshop is a hands-on session where participants learn about Internet of Things (IoT) technologies, devices, and applications.

VLSI

A VLSI workshop focuses on Very Large Scale Integration (VLSI) design, covering semiconductor technology, circuit design, and chip fabrication processes.
