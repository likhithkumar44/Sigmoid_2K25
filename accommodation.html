Read Before Registering for Accommodation

Accommodation will be provided in the hostel rooms of Sri Venkateswara University. Separate rooms for boys and girls will be allotted. Basic amenities will be available for bathing purposes. Students must carry their own bedsheets. Accommodation will be available on the night of Saturday, March 22, 2025. It includes dinner on March 22 and breakfast on March 23. You must register for accommodation in advance. On the morning of March 22, you can collect your registration pass and accommodation details at the registration desk. Only participants registered for Sigmoid are eligible for accommodation. Your belongings are your responsibility.


For any further queries, please contact the accommodation desk before registering.
