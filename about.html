FAQs

We've got the answers

Who can participate in the tect fest?

The tech fest is open to students with an intrest in technology. Participants from diverse backgrounds and disciplines are welcome to join.

Do I need to bring any materials or equipment for the competitions?

This website provides information about our services and products. You can find details about our offerings in the different sections.

How do I register from the tech fest?

To register for the Tech Fest, visit the official registration portal, fill in your details, select your pass, and submit the form with payment.

Are there any registration fees?

Yes, there are different registration passes available, each with a unique price; please refer to the registration portal for more details on the passes.

Can I participant in multiple events?

Yes, you can participate in multiple events according to the pass you purchased.

Will there be any prizes or awards for winners?

Yes, winners will receive exciting prizes and awards for their performance in the events.

Is there accommodation avaliable for outstation participants?

Yes, accommodation is available for outstation participants; please check the accomidation portal for more details.

Whom should I contact for furthur inquires or assistance?

For further inquiries or assistance, please contact the event coordinator; event coordinators details are present in "our team" portal in the Sigmoid website.
